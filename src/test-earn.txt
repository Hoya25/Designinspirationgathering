Testing Earn NCTR feature:

To access the Earn NCTR page:
1. Sign in or create an account (use any email/password)
2. Once on the Dashboard, look for the "Earn NCTR" button in two places:
   - In the NCTR Balance card at the top (blue "Earn More" button)
   - In the Quick Actions section on the right sidebar
3. Click either button to navigate to the Earn NCTR page

The Earn NCTR page includes:
- Category filters (All, Daily Tasks, Social, Referrals, Challenges, Partners)
- Earn opportunity cards showing:
  - Task name and description
  - NCTR reward amount
  - "Claim" or "Start" buttons
  - Completion tracking

Style improvements applied:
- Neutral color scheme (black buttons, neutral backgrounds)
- Pill-shaped filter buttons
- Cleaner card designs with better spacing
- Simplified typography
- Subtle hover effects
